Events Stack

Web Page Design

Unleash your creativity by designing innovative and visually striking web pages. Engage in hands-on sessions where you'll learn modern design techniques, coding practices, and interactive elements to create user-friendly, responsive websites.

Quiz

Test your knowledge and quick thinking in an exhilarating quiz challenge. Compete against your peers in a fast-paced contest covering a variety of topics, and showcase your intellectual prowess while having fun.

Paper Presentation

Present your research and innovative ideas in a formal setting. This event offers you the chance to share insights, receive expert feedback, and engage in meaningful discussions on emerging trends and breakthrough concepts

Spell Bee

Demonstrate your mastery over language in a high-energy spelling challenge. This event not only tests your vocabulary but also hones your focus and quick reflexes as you race against time and competitors.

Debate

Step into the arena of ideas and arguments. In this dynamic debate, you'll present and defend your viewpoints, engage with thought-provoking topics, and refine your public speaking and critical thinking skills in a competitive yet constructive environment..

Project Expo

Showcase your innovative projects and creative solutions at our Project Expo. This platform allows you to exhibit your work, network with industry experts, and gain valuable insights that could propel your ideas to the next level.

Shark Tank

Pitch your entrepreneurial ideas in a high-stakes, real-world simulation inspired by the popular TV show. Convince a panel of experienced investors with your innovative business proposals and strategic planning, and get ready to turn your vision into reality

Treasure Hunt

Embark on an adventurous quest filled with challenging puzzles and hidden clues. Navigate through a series of exciting tasks designed to test your problem-solving skills and team collaboration, all while searching for the ultimate treasure.

E-Games

Dive into the digital battleground with intense, adrenaline-pumping e-games. Compete in popular titles like Freefire and BGMI where strategy, quick reflexes, and teamwork lead to victory in the virtual arena.

Fun Games

Experience a variety of interactive and entertaining games that promise laughter and excitement. From quick challenges to engaging group activities, these games are designed to relax, entertain, and bring out your playful spirit.
